HOW WE WORK

An Intentional, Transparent Process

We divide our design timeline into structured checkpoints, ensuring constant collaboration, clear milestones, and zero unexpected surprises.

Average branding project duration:

3 to 5 Weeks

Start a Project
01

Kickoff & Discovery

We deep dive into your product market, target audiences, and business goals. Through collaborative boards, we define a strategic direction and visual theme before sketches begin.

02

Concept & Design System

We craft 2 to 3 distinct visual directions, presenting detailed design options complete with color themes, font variations, and logo applications on mockups so you can see it live.

03

Refinement & Feedback

We work in rapid iteration cycles based on your reviews. We refine the chosen concept, tightening font coordinates, logo vectors, grid systems, and layout sizes until polished.

04

Handoff & Launch Support

We package your brand assets into structured digital guidelines, including vector files (SVG, EPS, PDF), print checklists, fonts, custom palettes, and launch-ready elements.
